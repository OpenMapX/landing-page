This policy covers the OpenMapX marketing website at **openmapx.org**. The OpenMapX
application (openmapx.com) has its own in-app privacy notice.

## What we collect

This site is statically hosted and does not set advertising or tracking cookies. We collect
only aggregate, privacy-respecting server logs (timestamp, requested path, response status)
to keep the site running and secure.

## What we do not do

We do not sell data, run third-party ad trackers, or build advertising profiles.

Questions: privacy@openmapx.org
